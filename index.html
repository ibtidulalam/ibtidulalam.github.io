MacroCalculate

Description

MacroCalculate is an innovative Python-based application designed to empower users to take control of their daily nutrient intake. This sophisticated tool seamlessly integrates with the Nutritionix API, offering comprehensive nutritional data for a wide range of food items. Users can effortlessly log their meals, including breakfast, lunch, dinner, and snacks, and witness the exact breakdown of essential nutrients, such as calories, fat, protein, carbohydrates, vitamin C, and iron.

Usage

MacroCalculate is designed for simplicity and effectiveness. Upon launching the application, users are prompted to set their daily nutritional goals for the specified nutrients. They can then log their food consumption for each meal. The app, utilizing the Nutritionix API, searches for food items and allows users to specify the precise product and portion size.

Once all meals are recorded, MacroCalculate performs in-depth calculations to provide a comprehensive overview of the user's nutrient intake. This detailed summary not only displays the total nutrient consumption but also offers a clear comparison with the user's nutritional objectives. This enables users to easily monitor their dietary choices, make informed adjustments, and optimize their nutritional intake.

World Alarm

Description

This Python-based alarm notification app allows users to set location-based alarms and receive popup notifications on their Windows or macOS devices. The app supports both Windows and macOS platforms, providing a user-friendly way to schedule alarms for specific times in different time zones.

Features

  • Cross-platform

    The app works on both Windows and macOS, making it accessible to a wide range of users.

  • Location-Based Alarms

    Users can set alarms for specific locations using the IANA Time Zone Identifiers, such as 'America/Toronto' or 'Europe/Madrid'.

  • Non-Second Sensitive

    The app is hour and minute sensitive, allowing users to set alarms with a specified time without worrying about seconds.

  • Popup Notifications

    Alarms trigger popup notifications on the user's device when the specified time is reached.

  • Customizable

    Users can easily customize the location and time for their alarms by modifying a few variables in the script.
